Project Crevasse

3D Adventure / Unreal Engine

Project Overview

Crevasse is a high-stakes 3D survival adventure game. Players must swiftly climb icy cliffs before their body temperature drops to critical levels. The game combines strategic resource management with precision movement in a freezing, unforgiving environment.

Design & Management

Systems & Data

  • Data-Driven Design: Established an efficient variable management system using data tables to balance gameplay elements.
  • Difficulty Balancing: Configured specific difficulty parameters for each level via structured data tables for rapid iteration.

Creative Direction

  • UI/UX Design: Designed the core HUD and equipment icons, ensuring intuitive feedback for the survival mechanics.
  • Level Planning: Spearheaded the game's overall design and stage progression, focusing on the escalating challenge of the climb.
