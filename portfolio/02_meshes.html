Project Beast Crossing

Turn-Based Strategy / Hex-Grid

Project Overview

Beast Crossing is a competitive turn-based strategy game where two players command generals from the Squirrel, Snake, and Bird tribes. Players must strategically occupy territories and eliminate opponents through tactical movement and resource management on a hexagonal grid.

Technical Contributions

Core Mechanics

  • Turn Management: Engineered a robust TurnManager system to handle complex turn cycles and player actions.
  • Fog of War: Implemented a visibility system to restrict player's vision, adding tactical depth and exploration.
  • Pathfinding: Developed hexagonal tile movement logic and reachability calculations for unit navigation.

Graphics & Leadership

  • Hex-Tile System: Designed and implemented a mathematically precise hexagonal coordinate system and asset placement.
  • Project Management: Acted as the lead, managing task distribution and adjusting project scope to ensure timely delivery.
